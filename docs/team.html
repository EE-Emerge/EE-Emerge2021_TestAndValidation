EE-EMERGE 2021

EE-Emerge is a part of the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California, Davis. Over the course of a school year, sophomore and junior ECE students conceived, desgined, and built an electronic mask. EE-Emerge is sponsored by Texas Instruments.
